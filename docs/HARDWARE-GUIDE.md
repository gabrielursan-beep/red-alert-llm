- USB-A + USB-C dual connector (works everywhere without adapters)
- 1,050 MB/s read, 1,000 MB/s write
- 28.2g, keychain-sized (69.54 x 32.58 x 13.5 mm)
- Enough for: all models + full Wikipedia + encrypted vault + 500 GB free

### Alternatives

Any USB 3.0+ SSD or flash drive with 256 GB+ will work. Smaller/slower drives are compatible but will have longer model loading times and less space for knowledge bases.

### Not Recommended

- **Regular USB flash drives (USB 2.0)** — too slow (30 MB/s), model loading takes minutes
- **Hard disk drives (HDD)** — fragile, slow, heavy
- **SD cards** — too slow for model loading, no USB-A connector

## Computer Requirements

### Minimum
- **RAM:** 6 GB (for Qwen3-4B or Gemma-3-4B)
- **CPU:** Any 64-bit processor (x86_64 or ARM64)
- **USB:** USB 3.0 port (blue port)
- **OS:** Windows 10, macOS 12 (Monterey), Ubuntu 20.04

### Recommended
- **RAM:** 16 GB (for Qwen3-8B or 14B)
- **CPU:** Apple M1+ or Intel 12th gen+ or AMD Ryzen 5+
- **GPU:** Apple Metal (M1+) or NVIDIA RTX 3060+ (for CUDA acceleration)
- **USB:** USB 3.2 Gen 2 (10 Gbps)
- **OS:** Windows 11, macOS 14+, Ubuntu 22.04+

## Phone Requirements

### Android
- **RAM:** 6+ GB (4 GB minimum but slow)
- **USB:** USB-C with OTG support (most modern phones)
- **OS:** Android 8.0+
- **Storage:** 3+ GB free (to copy model from SSD)
- **Recommended phones:** Samsung Galaxy S23+, Pixel 7+, OnePlus 11+

### iOS
- **RAM:** 6+ GB recommended
- **USB:** USB-C (iPhone 15+) or Lightning with USB adapter
- **OS:** iOS 16+
- **Storage:** 3+ GB free
- **Recommended:** iPhone 15 Pro / Pro Max (8 GB RAM), iPad Air M2+

## USB-OTG Cables (for phones)

### USB-C phones (most modern phones)
The Kingston SSD connects directly — no adapter needed (it has USB-C).

### Micro-USB phones (older phones)
Need a Micro-USB to USB-A OTG adapter (~15-20 RON on eMAG.ro).

### Lightning iPhones (iPhone 14 and older)
Need Apple Lightning to USB Camera Adapter (~150 RON) or third-party adapter (~50 RON).
